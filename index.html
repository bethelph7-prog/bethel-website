About Our Church

Our Foundation

Our foundation is built upon Acts 2:42: "They devoted themselves to the apostles' teaching and to fellowship, to the breaking of bread and to prayer." This timeless passage guides everything we do as a church body.

We exist to glorify God by making disciples of Jesus Christ through unwavering devotion to biblical teaching, genuine fellowship, sacred communion, and fervent prayer.

The Apostles' Teaching

Deeply rooted in Scripture and sound doctrine, guiding our beliefs and lives. We are committed to faithful biblical exposition and teaching that transforms hearts and minds.

Fervent Fellowship

Building authentic, Christ-centered relationships and church bonds. We believe genuine connection happens when believers share life together in meaningful ways.

The Breaking of Bread

Commemorating Christ's sacrifice through communion and sharing fellowship meals. We honor the Lord's Table and gather around food to strengthen our bonds.

Unceasing Prayer

Seeking God's guidance, intervention, and presence in everything we do. Prayer is the foundation of our ministry and the source of our spiritual power and breakthrough.

Our Vision and Mission

Our Mission

At Bethel Prayer House, our mission is firmly rooted in the principles of Acts 2:42. We are committed to nurturing spiritual growth and serving sensory our community with Christ's transformative love and compassion, making disciples who impact the world.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a vibrant beacon of hope and transformation in Naperville and beyond. We envision creating a welcoming spiritual home where individuals and families can encounter God's grace and live out their divine purpose.

Indian International Student Ministry

Bethel Prayer House warmly welcomes Indian international students, especially those from the Telugu-speaking community. We understand the longing for home ‚Äì the familiar sights, sounds, and especially the taste of home-cooked Indian meals. We offer a spiritual haven and a family away from home, ensuring you can maintain your cultural and spiritual roots while thriving in America.

A Home Away From Home & Familiarity

Discover a vibrant, family-like church where you'll find the comfort of familiar Indian culture and the warmth of the Telugu community. We host regular fellowship events, celebrate Indian festivals, and foster strong friendships, ensuring you always feel connected and supported.

Academic & Career Guidance

Receive dedicated academic support and professional guidance from mentors who understand your journey. We offer valuable networking opportunities and programs designed to help you excel in your studies and successfully build your career path in America, with a church that cheers you on.

Practical Support

We offer essential practical assistance, from airport pickups and help with housing to transportation for local needs. Most importantly, we provide the comfort of home-cooked Indian meals and guidance for confidently navigating daily life in a new country.

Spiritual Nurturing

Stay deeply rooted in your faith and receive spiritual encouragement within a vibrant church that understands and celebrates your Indian heritage. Experience worship in a culturally familiar setting, providing solace and connection as you adjust to life in America.
